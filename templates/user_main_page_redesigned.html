Why Yumi

Emotional Intelligence

Yumi not only understands your needs, but also the emotions behind your words, and remembers the music, moments, and moods you care about the most.

As a result, every interaction is no longer cold and mechanical, but warm and natural, as if you were talking to a close friend.

Yumi is About

A Heart Station

Yumi is designed to be a 'heart station' for children and teenagers, especially those who lack parental care, have weak social support or live alone.

It supports both voice and text channels, patiently listening to and encouraging those who are introverted, inarticulate or easily misunderstood to speak their minds.

Through personalised memories and thoughtful reminders, it accompanies them through every critical moment of learning, living alone or growing up, and becomes an ever-reliable companion.
