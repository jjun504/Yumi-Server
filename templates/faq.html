Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Yumi AI Assistant

How can we help you?

Browse through our most frequently asked questions or search for specific topics

What is Yumi AI Assistant?

Yumi is an advanced AI assistant designed to provide meaningful connections and emotional support. It combines cutting-edge natural language processing with emotional intelligence to understand and respond to human emotions effectively.

Key features include:

  • Natural conversation capabilities
  • Emotional intelligence and empathy
  • Smart device integration (ESP-32 support)
  • Voice recognition and text-to-speech
  • Web-based interface for easy access

How do I get started with Yumi?

Getting started with Yumi is simple:

  • Step 1: Create an account on our platform
  • Step 2: Log in to access the console
  • Step 3: Start chatting with Yumi through the web interface
  • Step 4: Explore voice features and smart device integration

No additional software installation is required - everything runs through your web browser!

Is Yumi free to use?

Yes! Yumi is currently free to use during our beta phase. We believe in making AI assistance accessible to everyone.

Our free tier includes:

  • 100+ conversations/day
  • Multiple voice features
  • Smart device integration
  • Web interface access

We may introduce premium features in the future, but core functionality will always remain free.

Does Yumi support voice interaction?

Yes! Yumi supports both voice input and output:

  • Speech Recognition: Powered by Azure for accurate voice-to-text conversion
  • Text-to-Speech: Natural-sounding voice responses
  • Real-time Processing: Fast response times for smooth conversations
  • Multiple Languages: Support for Mandarin and English

Simply click the microphone button in the chat interface to start voice interaction.

Can Yumi control smart devices?

Absolutely! Yumi has built-in support for smart device integration:

  • ESP-32 Support: Direct control of ESP-32 based devices
  • Real-time Control: Instant device response and status updates
  • Custom Configurations: Set up device relationships and automation

Use the Control Panel to configure your devices and create custom automation rules.

What makes Yumi's AI different?

Yumi stands out through several key innovations:

  • Emotional Intelligence: Advanced algorithms to recognize and respond to emotions
  • Groq API Integration: Lightning-fast response times for smooth conversations
  • Contextual Understanding: Maintains conversation context for meaningful interactions
  • Personalization: Learns from interactions to provide better responses
  • Multi-modal Interface: Combines text, voice, and device control seamlessly

What technology powers Yumi?

Yumi is built on a modern, scalable technology stack:

  • Backend: Python Flask server with real-time processing
  • AI Engine: Groq API for fast language model inference
  • Speech: Azure Text-to-Speech for voice capabilities
  • Frontend: Modern web technologies with responsive design
  • IoT: ESP-32 integration for smart device control
  • Architecture: Dedicated server connected by multiple clients

Is my data secure with Yumi?

Yes, we take data security very seriously:

  • Privacy: Conversations are processed securely and not be viewed
  • Local Processing: Many operations happen locally on your device
  • No Tracking: We don't track or sell your personal information

Read our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

What browsers are supported?

Yumi works on all modern web browsers:

  • Chrome: Version 80+ (recommended)
  • Firefox: Version 75+ (developing)
  • Safari: Version 13+ (developing)
  • Edge: Version 80+ (developing)

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome.

Yumi is not responding. What should I do?

If Yumi is not responding, try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Check Connection: Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Refresh Page: Try refreshing your browser page
  • Clear Cache: Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Check Browser: Make sure you're using a supported browser
  • Restart Browser: Close and reopen your browser

If the problem persists, please contact our support team.

Voice features are not working. How can I fix this?

Voice feature issues are usually related to browser permissions:

  • Check Connection: Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Check Permissions: Go to browser settings and enable wake word function
  • Check Volume: Make sure your microphone volume is adequate
  • Environment: Make sure your environment is not quite noisy

In Chrome, click the microphone icon in the address bar to manage permissions.

Smart device connection issues

If you're having trouble connecting smart devices:

  • Device Status: Verify your ESP-32 device is powered and connected
  • MQTT Topic: Make sure you register device ID correctly in Control Panel
  • Configuration: Double-check device configuration in the Control Panel
  • Restart Device: Try restarting your smart device

Use the device discovery feature in the Control Panel to automatically detect devices.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is quick and easy:

  • Step 1: Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
  • Step 2: Enter your email address and choose a password
  • Step 3: Verify your email address
  • Step 4: Complete your profile setup

No credit card or payment information is required for the free tier.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

To reset your password:

  • Step 1: Go to the login page
  • Step 2: Click "Forgot Password?"
  • Step 3: Enter your email address
  • Step 4: Check your email for reset instructions
  • Step 5: Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
