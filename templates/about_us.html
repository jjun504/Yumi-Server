Our Objective

We create emotional AI companions

We hope that every user can feel companionship and caring from Yumi. Even if the simplest greeting, he/she will respond with emotional language such as: “A brand new day’s here! Don’t forget to head out with a big smile~!”.

Our mission is to develop AI companions that understand and respond to human emotions in meaningful ways. We believe technology should enhance human connection, not replace it. Yumi is designed to provide comfort, reduce loneliness, and offer a safe space for expression without judgment.

We envision a world where AI companions like Yumi help people feel understood and supported in their daily lives. Our goal is to continue advancing emotional AI technology that adapts to individual needs and provides personalized companionship that grows more meaningful over time.

We value empathy, privacy, and continuous learning. We're committed to creating AI companions that respect boundaries, protect user data, and evolve through genuine interactions. We believe emotional intelligence is as important as logical intelligence in creating truly helpful AI.
