Smart Home Control Hub

Tell Yumi about your smart home devices — whether they're inputs or outputs — and it will remember them all.

When you say "Turn on the lights," Yumi knows exactly which device to trigger. Even more, if you've connected sensors or input devices, Yumi can automatically respond — for example, turning on a fan when a motion sensor sends a "true" signal.

  • Remembers all your smart devices
  • Responds to voice commands instantly
  • Automatically links input signals to output actions
  • Lets you monitor everything via the website
  • Gives you full control with zero hassle
